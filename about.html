Pet Salon

Your pet, our passion.

There’s nothing like snuggling with your pets when they’re soft and clean and smell nice. We offer everything you need to keep your pet clean, healthy, and comfortable at the Pet Salon. From a simple bath to an elaborate grooming treatment, our expert groomers focus on creating a calm and relaxing experience your pet can enjoy. Enrichment activities can be added to help your pet relax even more and during their spa day.

All pets are unique. Just like humans, your pet deserves personalized grooming for their size, breed, coat type, and condition. For this reason, prices, products, and services vary. Please ask an associate for details.
